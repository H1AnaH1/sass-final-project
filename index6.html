Day 1: Tbilisi – Zugdidi

Our driver will pick you up from your hotel at 20:00 and take you to the Central train station in Tbilisi. The overnight train to Zugdidi leaves at 21:10 and arrives in Zugdidi at 06:25 the next morning.

Day 2: Zugdidi – Mestia

Our driver will meet you at Zugdidi train station to drive you up to Mestia (about 130 km), one of the oldest towns in Svaneti region of Georgia. This region is a birthplace of the svans, a unique ethnic group, who distinguish with their amazing and old culture and traditions. Svaneti is also famous with its original and authentic architecture and is dubbed as a “land of thousand towers”. Those towers used to play an important economic and defensive role in the history of Svaneti. There are still dozens of the towers – Middle Ages stone houses with fire watchtowers; and churches (10-14th cc). In the evening visit Mestia Ethnographic museum, original svan house “machubi”, and “koshki” tower. Dinner and overnight at the hotel or local guesthouse.

Day 3: Mestia – Ushguli – Mestia

After the hotel breakfast drive to Ushguli village, the highest standing settlement in Europe (2200 m). Ushguli architectural complex is a valuable architectural and historical monument listed in the World heritage of UNESCO for its exclusiveness. There are about 70 families living in Ushguli (around 200 people). Spend time in Ushguli strolling old narrow streets. Visit Lamaria Church complex with impressive 12th century wall frescos. Locals believe that Queen Tamar was buried under this church. Enjoy picturesque view of the Mount Shkhara. Return to Mestia to enjoy delicious dinner made of Svan traditional dishes.

Day 4: Mestia – Zugdidi – Tbilisi

After breakfast check-out from hotel. Free time to walk around Mestia until afternoon drive back to Zugdidi. Upon arrival visit the Dadiani Palace (Western Georgia's royal family). Drive to Zugdidi train station to take an overnight train back to Tbilisi. Departure at 22:15, arrival on the next morning at 06:30. Transfer to your hotel.
